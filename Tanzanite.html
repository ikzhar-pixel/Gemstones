About Tanzanite

A rare blue-violet stone, found only in Tanzania

Tanzanite Characteristics

Hardness

Tanzanite is not very hard — about 6 to 6.5 on the Mohs scale. This stone needs to be handled with care

Transparency

Most tanzanites are transparent. High-quality stones have excellent clarity, with few visible inclusions

Sparkle

Tanzanite has a bright, glass-like shine. When well-cut, it reflects light beautifully and can look quite dazzling

Color

Tanzanite is famous for its vibrant blue to violet shades. It can also show flashes of purple or even depending on the angle

Clarity

Good tanzanites are mostly clean, but some small inclusions are normal. Clear stones with rich color are especially valuable

Origin

Tanzanite is found only in one place on Earth — near Mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania. That’s why it’s so rare and special

About Tanzanite

Some Info

Tanzanite is a rare and beautiful gemstone found only in Tanzania. It is known for its stunning blue-violet color and unique sparkle. Tanzanite is softer than many other gems, so it should be handled with care. Because of its limited origin, it is considered one of the most precious and unique gemstones in the world.
