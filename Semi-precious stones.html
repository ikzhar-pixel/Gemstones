About Semi-Precious Stones

Some Information

Semi-precious gemstones are more common than precious ones but still very beautiful. Examples include amethyst, garnet, turquoise, topaz, and peridot. They come in many colors and are used in many types of jewelry. Although they are less expensive, some semi-precious stones can still be rare and valuable.

Some Semi-Precious Stones

For example

Amethyst

A purple variety of quartz. A symbol of calm, wisdom, and protection. In ancient times, it was believed to protect against drunkenness and evil thoughts

Obsidian

Black volcanic glass. It has sharp edges, so it was once used for tools. A symbol of strength, protection, and deep emotions

Malachite

A bright green stone with beautiful patterns. Not very hard, but very decorative. Considered a stone of protection and transformation
