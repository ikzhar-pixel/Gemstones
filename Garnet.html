About Garnet

Garnet is a gemstone known for its vibrant colors, ranging from red to green

Garnet Characteristics

Hardness

Garnet has a hardness of 6.5 to 7.5 on the Mohs scale. It's fairly durable and can resist scratches

Transparency

Garnets are usually transparent to translucent. The more transparent the stone, the higher its value

Sparkle

Garnet has a nice glassy shine (vitreous luster). When cut well, it reflects light beautifully

Color

Most garnets are deep red, but they can also be orange, green, yellow, purple, and even black

Clarity

High-quality garnets are usually clear with few inclusions. Some types of garnets, are especially prized when flawless.

Origin

Garnets are found all over the world — in countries like India, Sri Lanka, the USA, and Madagascar.

About Garnet

Some Info

Garnet is a beautiful gemstone known for its deep red color, though it also comes in many other shades like green, orange, and purple. It has been used in jewelry for thousands of years. Garnet symbolizes love, energy, and protection. Because of its strength and sparkle, it’s a popular choice for rings, necklaces, and other accessories.
