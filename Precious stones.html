About Gemstones

Some information

Precious gemstones are the rarest and most valuable types of stones. The most famous ones are diamonds, rubies, sapphires, and emeralds. They are known for their hardness, vibrant colors, and brilliance. These gems are often used in high-end jewelry and are symbols of wealth and status.
