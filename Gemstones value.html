Gemstones value

Gemstones are valued for their rarity, beauty, hardness, clarity, and color

Gemstones are Highly valued Because of

Rarity

The rarer the stone is in nature, the more valuable it becomes. Some gemstones are found in only one place on Earth

Beauty

A gemstone's brilliance, sparkle, and light reflection can make it visually stunning. Some stones even show special effects like color change

Hardness

Harder stones are more resistant to scratches and damage, which makes them suitable for everyday jewelry like rings

Clarity and Purity

Stones with fewer internal flaws or inclusions are clearer and shine better, making them more expensive

Color

Intense and even color tones, especially rare colors like deep red or vivid green, can greatly increase a gemstoneâ€™s value

Size

Larger gemstones are harder to find and usually worth more, especially if they maintain good clarity and color
