Diamond Characteristics

Hardness

Diamond is the hardest natural mineral in the world. Its hardness is 10 on the Mohs scale. This means it can scratch any other stone, but it is almost impossible to scratch

Transparency

Diamonds can be completely transparent or have a slight tint (such as yellowish, blue, or pink). The clearer and more transparent the diamond, the more valuable it is

Sparkle

Diamonds have a unique, intense sparkle called "adamantine luster". This type of shine is stronger than in any other gemstone. When light enters a diamond, it bends

Clarity

Perfect diamonds have no cracks, scratches, or inclusions. Such stones are very rare and expensive
