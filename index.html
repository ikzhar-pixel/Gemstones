Types of Gemstones and Where they are Mined

Precious stones

These rare gems like diamonds and rubies are highly valuable and used in jewelry

Semi-precious stones

More common stones like amethyst and topaz, loved for their beauty and colors

Stone mining

Gemstones are mined from the Earth using tunnels, open pits, or found in rivers

Reasons why Gemstones are expensive

Gemstones are valued for:

Rarity – rare stones are more valuable
Beauty – shine, color, and sparkle matter
Hardness – harder gems last longer and resist scratches
Clarity – fewer flaws mean higher quality
Color – bright, rich colors are most prized
Size – bigger stones are rarer and more expensive

Top 6 Most Expensive Gemstones in the World

Diamond

The hardest stone in the world. Shiny, transparent, and very valuable. A symbol of eternity

Ruby

Ruby is one of the most valuable gemstones. Its rich red color symbolizes love, energy, and power

Emerald

Emerald is one of the most famous and beautiful gemstones with a bright green color. It is valued for its depth of color

Sapphire

Usually blue, but can come in other colors. A symbol of wisdom

Alexandrite

Changes color: green in daylight, and violet or red under lamp light

Tanzanite

A rare blue-violet stone, found only in Tanzania
